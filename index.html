Our Story

BUILT FROM THE
STEEL CITY

Born in the industrial heart of Mandi Gobindgarh — India's steel capital — Bombay Gobindgarh Roadways was forged in the same fire as the products we transport. From a single-truck operation to a multi-fleet logistics powerhouse, we've earned the trust of India's leading manufacturers.

Our mission is simple: move cargo faster, safer, and smarter. We combine industrial-grade reliability with modern trip management systems — giving our partners real-time visibility from loading dock to delivery point.

What We Move

OUR SERVICES

Full-spectrum logistics services tailored for India's industrial and FMCG sectors.

01

FULL TRUCK LOAD

Dedicated FTL shipments — single-client trucks running point-to-point with no co-loading. Maximum speed, zero detours.

02

PART TRUCK LOAD

Cost-effective LTL/PTL options for smaller consignments with consolidated routing and shared transport solutions.

03

HEAVY CARGO

Flatbed, low-bed, and open-body transport for steel coils, machinery, alloys, and oversized industrial equipment.

04

FMCG DISTRIBUTION

High-frequency distribution runs for fast-moving consumer goods — snacks, beverages, packaged foods — to warehouses and retail hubs.

05

REVERSE LOGISTICS

Return shipment management, empty container repositioning, and damaged goods recovery across the network.

06

DIGITAL TRIP MGMT

Real-time trip tracking, digital proof-of-delivery, driver check-ins, and automated reporting for complete supply chain visibility.
